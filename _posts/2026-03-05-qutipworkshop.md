During **April 2–20, 2026**, we will host QuTiP developers at **RIKEN (Japan)** for another QuTiP Development Workshop.  Anyone can participate by contacting the organizers at qutip-admin@googlegroups.com, regardless of their field of work.

With a focus on feature development for **QuTiP v6 and beyond**, we are planning a core week of presentations and development discussions during **April 8–15**.

## Preliminary Presentation Schedule
