From **April 2–20, 2026**, we will host QuTiP developers at **RIKEN (Japan)** for another QuTiP Development Workshop.

With a focus on feature development for **QuTiP v6 and beyond**, we are planning a core week of presentations and development discussions from **April 8–15**.

## Preliminary Presentation Schedule

April 2-7th is currently reserved for arrival and issue hacking days. For the core-week, we have the following schedule.
